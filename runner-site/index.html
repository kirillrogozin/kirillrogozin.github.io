I am a trail runner and invite you to join my community

Why

I love trail running because I can run everywhere: through forests, fields, hills, past lakes, rivers. Such a diversity of profiles and sights makes running an exciting exercise. It clears your mind, makes your body and life as a whole healthier and more exciting.

How exactly

Want to start running? Easy. No catch. :-) Just start running by time: say, 10, 15, 20 minutes. Increase the time gradually. Your leg muscles will start adapting to the new workload and you will feel yourself more confident while covering longer distances.

Equipment

I love running because you don't need to have much equipment to start the sport. All you need is:

  • a pair of running shoes
  • a T-shirt or a shirt
  • shorts or tights
  • a cap or a bandana and sunglasses
  • a fanny bag with small water bottle holder

Once you progress enough to start running for a one, two or more hours you will need a running backpack which allows you to have more equipment with you:

  • a hydration system (a "bladder" in a backpack to have more water with you)
  • gels to feed you with carbohydrates and give energy
  • a flashlight to run in twilight or at night
  • a raincoat
  • a medical kit and rescue blanket
  • any other stuff depending on the conditions (mountains, forests, etc)
